Graduate Capstone

Google, ChatGPT and Digital Marketing.

"Utilizing the Uses and Gratifications Theory to Explain How Users’ Relationships With Google’s Existing Digital Advertising Structure Will Change With The Integration of ChatGPT-like Technology in Search Engines"

To complete his graduate program, Ben researched, wrote, and submitted an in-depth dissertation on the launch of OpenAI's ChatGPT and the ramifications such technology would have on the digital marketing sphere. In this Capstone project, Ben explicates how the specific search query answers of ChatGPT and BingChat threaten Google's position as an online advertising brokerage.
