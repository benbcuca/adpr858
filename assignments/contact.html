Resume Highlights

Born in Lincoln, Nebraska, Ben is a Cornhusker at heart. However, Ben attended the Williston Northampton School in Massachusetts for his high school education. Ben then matriculated to Trinity College, receiving a B.A. in English Literature with a minor in Creative writing. After graduating from Trinity, Ben began his freelance career in New York City. During the COVID-19 Pandemic, Ben returned to Lincoln, where he started his graduate studies at the University of Nebraska. Ben is expected to graduate in May 2023 with a Master's in Communications and Marketing.

Ben is an educated communications specialist excited by data's power to deliver marketing results. Ben has a history of cultivating successful relationships with senior leadership, cross-functional teams, and clients. Possesses comprehensive data research and analysis skills used to develop creative marketing initiatives capable of influencing brand positioning, client acquisitions, and sales generation.
